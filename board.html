Leadership

Board of Directors

The individuals who guide our mission with integrity and dedication.

The Lion and Sun Foundation is governed by a Board of Directors committed to upholding the highest standards of humanitarian leadership, ethical governance, and institutional independence. Each member brings expertise, dedication, and a shared vision for a better future.

Dr. Layla Ahmadi

Chair of the Board

International humanitarian law specialist with over two decades of experience in crisis response, institutional governance, and human rights advocacy across the Middle East and Europe.

Mr. Dariush Karimi

Vice Chair & Secretary General

Former senior diplomat and public policy advisor with extensive experience in international development, multilateral institutions, and civil society engagement across four continents.

Prof. Shirin Moradi

Director of Programs

Professor of public health and epidemiology, recognized for her field research in post-conflict healthcare reconstruction and community-based rehabilitation models.

Mr. Farhad Tehrani

Chief Financial Officer

Certified financial auditor and non-profit governance expert with a distinguished career overseeing transparent fiscal management for international humanitarian organizations.

Dr. Nasrin Hosseini

Director of Child Welfare

Pediatric psychologist and child protection advocate, specializing in trauma-informed care, educational inclusion programs, and the psychosocial support of displaced children.

Eng. Reza Sadeghi

Director of Infrastructure

Civil engineer and urban development specialist with 25 years of experience leading infrastructure reconstruction projects in conflict-affected and disaster-stricken communities.

Governance

Independent & Accountable

The Board of Directors operates under a strict governance framework designed to ensure full independence, financial accountability, and the highest ethical standards. Board decisions are made collectively, in alignment with the Foundation's humanitarian mandate, and are subject to independent audit and public reporting.
