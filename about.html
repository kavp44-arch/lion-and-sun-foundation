Our Mission

A Humanitarian Foundation
Built on Principle

The Lion and Sun Foundation is dedicated to supporting the Iranian people through concrete humanitarian initiatives focused on children, healthcare, environmental well-being, and the reconstruction of essential services. Our mission is guided by compassion, responsibility, and an unwavering commitment to transparency.


Founded on the belief that every individual deserves dignity, safety, and the opportunity for a better future, the Foundation channels its efforts into areas of greatest need — prioritizing the most vulnerable members of society, including children, victims of violence, and communities deprived of basic services.

We believe that meaningful and lasting change emerges not from isolated acts of charity, but from systemic, well-governed, and transparently executed programs that build resilience and restore human potential. The Lion and Sun Foundation operates at this intersection of urgent need and long-term transformation.

What Guides Us

Our Core Values

Compassion

We place human dignity at the center of all our actions. Every program, every initiative, and every decision is guided by empathy for those who suffer and a genuine desire to alleviate that suffering.

Responsibility

We hold ourselves accountable to the communities we serve, to our partners, and to the public. We take our obligations seriously and act with the highest standards of ethical conduct in all circumstances.

Transparency

Trust is the foundation of humanitarian credibility. We maintain rigorous financial oversight, clear reporting mechanisms, and open communication — ensuring that all resources are directed toward tangible, measurable impact.

Our Mandate

Independence & Humanitarian Mandate

"The Lion and Sun Foundation operates with complete independence and a strictly humanitarian mandate. Our actions are guided by principles of transparency, accountability, and ethical governance — free from political influence, and entirely dedicated to the well-being of the people we serve."

The Foundation's independence is not merely a structural characteristic — it is a core operational principle. We do not align with any political party, government, or ideological movement. Our sole allegiance is to the humanitarian imperative: the protection and advancement of human dignity and well-being.

This independence allows us to act impartially, to direct resources where they are most needed, and to build the trust necessary to operate effectively in complex and challenging environments. It is a commitment we renew with every decision we make.
